Monetization Audit Tool

Ranjan Dasgupta is building a client-side tool that will help publishers and developers analyze their monetization setup — header bidding, supply path, and yield — without sending your data to a server. Coming soon.

What This Tool Will Analyze

Header Bidding & Prebid

Detect Prebid.js and other header bidding wrappers, adapter presence, timeout settings, and request flow so you can compare your setup to best practices.

Supply Path & Demand

Identify demand partners and paths present on the page, and surface potential supply path optimization (SPO) opportunities.

Ad Units & Floors

Review ad unit structure, sizes, and floor-related signals where visible from the client, to support yield and floor strategy decisions.

Performance & Latency

Measure the impact of ad requests on page load and Core Web Vitals, so you can balance revenue and user experience.

Privacy

This tool will run entirely in your browser. No URLs or page data will be sent to Ranjan Dasgupta or AdsGupta servers. All analysis is client-side only.
