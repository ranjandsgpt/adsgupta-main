FAQs

What is Prebid and why does it matter for publishers?
Prebid is an open-source header bidding solution that lets publishers run a single auction across multiple demand partners before calling the ad server. It increases competition and typically lifts revenue by allowing more bidders to compete for each impression.
How do you optimize Prebid timeouts and latency?
Ranjan Dasgupta tunes Prebid timeouts based on device type, geography, and number of bidders. Shorter timeouts reduce page latency; longer timeouts can capture more bids. The goal is to balance fill rate, CPM, and user experience—often with staggered timeouts per adapter.
What is a unified auction (GAM + Prebid)?
A unified auction combines header bidding demand (Prebid) with Google Ad Manager’s waterfall in a single, first-price auction. The highest bid from all sources wins. This avoids the traditional waterfall and usually improves revenue and transparency.
