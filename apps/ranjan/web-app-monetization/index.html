Unified Web & App Monetization

For modern publishers and platforms, users move fluidly between Web and App. Monetization strategies that treat these environments separately often leave money on the table and complicate product decisions. Ranjan Dasgupta works on unifying Web and App monetization so that demand, pricing, and measurement are aligned.

At Glance, he helped build an end-to-end stack that spans lockscreen experiences, in-app content, and Web properties. This stack coordinated header bidding, SDK demand, and direct campaigns into a single, comprehensible system that could be tuned through experimentation.

Ranjan’s work extends into CTV via CTV monetization, bringing the same principles of unified design and measurement into emerging screen types. The goal is simple: one strategy and one source of truth for monetization, regardless of where users are.

Approach to Web & App Monetization

Architecture and SDK Design

SDKs and tags are the plumbing of monetization. Ranjan works with engineering teams to design lightweight, extensible SDKs that can adapt to new formats and partners without constant rewrites.

Demand and Pricing Strategy

Demand partners should not compete chaotically across Web and App. Ranjan coordinates partner strategy and yield optimization practices so that each partner’s role is clear and pricing remains coherent.

Measurement and Experimentation

Scaling monetization requires strong measurement foundations: unified reporting, clear KPIs, and a culture of experimentation. Ranjan helps build pipelines and dashboards that make it possible to run controlled tests across platforms.

Cross-Platform User and UX Considerations

Monetization cannot come at the cost of user trust. Ranjan ensures that ad load, formats, and placements are tuned to each surface while respecting overall user experience goals.

Web & App Monetization FAQs

What is Web and App monetization at scale?
It is the practice of running a unified monetization strategy that spans browser and native environments, with consistent pricing, partner management, and reporting. For Ranjan, scale means not just revenue, but operational stability and clarity.
How has Ranjan Dasgupta scaled Web & App monetization?
Ranjan has worked with teams at Glance and InMobi to architect, launch, and refine large-scale monetization systems, coordinating everything from SDKs and tags to programmatic exchanges and direct sales.
Should Web and App use the same partners?
Not always, but it is important that the portfolio of partners makes sense as a whole. Ranjan evaluates each partner’s strengths across platforms and uses SPO principles to keep supply efficient.
How can teams improve their current setup?
Teams can begin by mapping their full stack, identifying duplicated efforts, and unifying metrics. From there, Ranjan recommends a roadmap of architectural and yield changes prioritized by impact and risk.
