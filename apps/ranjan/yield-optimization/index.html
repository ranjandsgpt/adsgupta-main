Ranjan Dasgupta helps publishers design yield optimization frameworks that go beyond quick fixes, connecting ad server configuration, header bidding, and supply path optimization. For more, see Insights on programmatic advertising and AdTech.

Yield Optimization Strategy

For many publishers, yield optimization has historically meant “raise floors until something breaks.” Ranjan Dasgupta brings a more disciplined approach, rooted in experiment design and clear success metrics. He helps teams understand how changes in pricing, packaging, and demand routing impact net revenue and user outcomes.

Having worked across Google, Automatad, Glance, and InMobi, Ranjan has seen yield optimization from both vendor and publisher perspectives. This gives him a nuanced view of how ad servers like GAM, header bidding systems like Prebid, and exchange behavior all interact to produce final results.

Ranjan also pays close attention to cross-platform effects. Web and App monetization are often managed by separate teams, but users and advertisers do not experience them in isolation. Aligning strategies across screens is a key part of his Web & App monetization at scale work.

Approach to Publisher Yield

Floor Price Design and Governance

Floors can be powerful, but without governance they quickly become brittle. Ranjan helps define policies around base floors, dynamic floors, and rule-based pricing that keep setups understandable and testable.

Demand Stack Optimization

Yield optimization is as much about who gets access to inventory as what they pay. Ranjan works with teams to rationalize demand partners, align them with programmatic advertising strategy, and remove underperforming paths that add complexity without revenue.

Experimentation and A/B Testing

Instead of relying on intuition alone, Ranjan encourages publishers to build robust experimentation muscle. This includes thoughtful control groups, adequate run times, and honest interpretation of sometimes noisy results.

Connecting Yield with SPO and Header Bidding

Yield decisions ripple through header bidding and buyer-side SPO. By coordinating with header bidding optimization and SPO frameworks, Ranjan ensures that yield gains do not come at the expense of buyer trust or marketplace health.

Yield Optimization FAQs

What is yield optimization for publishers?
Yield optimization is the ongoing process of maximizing revenue per impression across all demand sources. It factors in pricing, demand configuration, auction mechanics, and user experience to produce sustainable results.
How does Ranjan Dasgupta approach yield optimization?
Ranjan starts with a clear baseline and then designs a roadmap of experiments across floors, demand mix, and header bidding behavior. He measures impact using revenue, latency, and retention metrics.
How do floors and fill rate interact?
Higher floors can drive higher eCPMs but may reduce fill, especially in softer markets. Ranjan helps teams model these trade-offs so that decisions are made from data rather than guesswork.
How does yield optimization relate to SPO?
Yield and supply path optimization are two sides of the same coin. Yield decisions affect how buyers perceive inventory quality and efficiency, while SPO decisions influence which paths deliver the best economics.
